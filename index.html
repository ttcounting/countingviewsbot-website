CountingViewsBot

CountingViewsBot is a Telegram bot for counting views of YouTube and TikTok videos. It collects the title, number of views, and publication date.

Features

  • Track YouTube video views
  • Track TikTok video views
  • Get video title and publication date
  • Save data to Google Sheets
