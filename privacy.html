Privacy Policy - CountingViewsBot

1. Information Collection
The bot only collects public information about YouTube and TikTok videos: title, view count, and publication date. No personal user data is collected.

2. Use of Information
Collected data is used only to display information in Telegram and to save it in a Google Sheet.

3. Data Storage
Data is stored in a Google Sheet, accessible only to the bot owner.

4. Changes
The Privacy Policy may be updated without notice.
